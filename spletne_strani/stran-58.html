      Please type in the title and select the platform for the game you wish to add. Please make sure the title matches the game's box art as closely as possible, using colons and not dashes.

      Some examples

      • King's Quest VI: Heir Today, Gone Tomorrow
      • Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance
      • Star Wars Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II

      Some examples of incorrect titles

      • DOOM III [does not match box art]
      • Addams Family, The: Pugsley's Scavenger Hunt [words are out of order]
      • King's Quest 6: Heir Today, Gone Tomorrow [the use of "6" does not match the box art]

      • Star Wars: Jedi Knight [part of the title is missing]
