Overview

When the original T-Virus was finally suppressed following a horrific and still hushed-up outbreak in a remote mansion, a disaster seemed to have been averted. But now the nightmare has returned. A new super-strain has emerged and terror stalks the streets of isolated Raccoon City in Resident Evil 2. The whole town is infested and blood thirsty zombies run wild. It's not pretty. Two outsiders, Leon, a cop, and Claire, a reporter, are the only two to escape contamination and the only ones capable of saving Raccoon City from total destruction - and preventing the spread of the new virus around the world. You'll face wave after wave of undead mutants intent on ripping out your heart and soul - but only after you've faced your own fear down first. Exclusive PC features include: • Both European and Japanese versions included! • High Resolution 3D character graphics • New Hidden Extreme Battle Game Mode to discover • Resident Evil Gallery (view pictures, movies and 3D objects from the game)
