Overview

Raise Hell! Dante's past is revealed as Devil May Cry returns to its roots. Master Dante's multiple fighting styles while battling never before seen demons and new characters as you fight your way towards a brutal confrontation with Dante's mysterious twin brother Vergil. Choose Dante's Fighting Style - maximize the impact of close-up blade attacks or blow enemies away from a distance with a huge arsenal of weapons including handguns, shotguns, swords and more. Battle against a a horde of demonic enemies that are more ferocious than ever with powerful weapons and the abilities to transform and fly.
