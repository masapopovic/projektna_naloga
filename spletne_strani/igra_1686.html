Overview

Explore the pleasures and horrors of love as Vincent, a man with a hard choice to make: marry his long-time girlfriend Katherine or move on to the incredible blonde he just woke up next to — named Catherine! But beware! Make the wrong choice, and you could end up dead. Delving into themes of free will and the delicate nature of relationships and the choices we make, this action-adventure-puzzle game is an experience unlike anything else. As the name suggests, this was a PC release of the original Catherine game, named as such due to the fact that an updated re-release, Catherine: Full Body, was released in the same time period, and would not debut on Steam until much later.
